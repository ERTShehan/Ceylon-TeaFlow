About Ceylon TeaFlow

Ceylon TeaFlow is a comprehensive management system designed specifically for tea factories in Sri Lanka. Our platform streamlines the entire tea production process from leaf to cup, providing tools for every stakeholder in the supply chain.

From Garden to Factory

Track tea leaves from suppliers through quality assessment, processing, and inventory management.

Business Intelligence

Gain insights with comprehensive analytics on production, sales, inventory, and financial performance.

Multi-Role Platform

Customized dashboards for CEOs, Suppliers, Tea Makers, Managers, and Customers.

Why Choose Ceylon TeaFlow?

Our system is specifically designed for the unique needs of Sri Lanka's tea industry, offering:

  • Real-time tracking of tea leaf quality and quantity
  • Integrated financial management for payments and expenses
  • Customized portals for each type of user
  • Comprehensive reporting and analytics
  • Secure, cloud-based access from anywhere

Account Types

Ceylon TeaFlow offers different account types tailored to specific roles in the tea industry. Choose the one that matches your needs.

Supplier Account

For tea leaf suppliers

Benefits:

  • Track your tea leaf deliveries and quality assessments
  • Monitor payments and account balance
  • Schedule future deliveries
  • View your supply history and performance metrics
  • Access to specialized supplier support

Registration Process:

1
Obtain Tea Card

Visit the factory to register as a supplier and receive your unique tea card number.

2
Online Registration

Select "Supplier" during account creation and enter your tea card number.

3
Account Verification

Factory management will verify your account within 24-48 hours.

4
Full Access

Once verified, gain full access to the supplier portal.

Register as Supplier

Customer Account

For tea product customers

Benefits:

  • Browse and purchase premium Ceylon tea products
  • Track order status and delivery information
  • Access order history and receipts
  • Earn loyalty rewards with purchases
  • Receive notifications about new tea varieties

Registration Process:

1
Select Account Type

Choose "Customer" during the registration process.

2
Provide Information

Enter your personal details and contact information.

3
Email Verification

Verify your email address through the link we send you.

4
Start Shopping

Immediately access our tea catalog and place orders.
